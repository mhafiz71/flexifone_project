Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a payment on my credit account?
You can make payments through your dashboard by clicking on "Manage Payments" in the Quick Actions section. We accept all major credit cards and bank transfers.
What happens if I miss a payment?
If you miss a payment, a late fee may be applied to your account. Multiple missed payments could affect your credit score. Please contact us immediately if you anticipate payment difficulties.
Can I pay off my credit account early?
Yes, you can pay off your credit account at any time without any early repayment fees. Simply use the "Manage Payments" option to make a full payment of your remaining balance.
How do I update my personal information?
You can update your personal information by going to your account settings. If you need to change critical information like your name or national ID, please contact our support team directly.
