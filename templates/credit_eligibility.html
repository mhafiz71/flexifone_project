Credit Eligibility

Understanding how our credit system works

How Credit Eligibility Works

At FlexiFone, we believe in making technology accessible to everyone through flexible payment options. Our credit system is designed to be transparent and fair.

Basic Requirements

  • Account Verification: Your account must be verified by our team to ensure security.
  • Minimum Credit Score: A credit score of at least 600 is required.

Credit Assessment Factors

When you apply for credit, we evaluate several factors to determine your eligibility:

Credit Score

Your credit score reflects your credit history and payment behavior. A higher score increases your chances of approval.

Income Verification

We assess your monthly income to ensure you can comfortably manage the installment payments.

Debt-to-Income Ratio

We look at your existing financial obligations compared to your income to ensure responsible lending.

Employment Status

Stable employment helps demonstrate your ability to make regular payments.

Credit Score Calculation

Our credit scoring system takes into account:

  • Base Score (40%): Your existing credit score
  • Income Score (30%): Based on your monthly income
  • Debt-to-Income Score (30%): Lower debt-to-income ratios receive higher scores

Approval Criteria

To be approved for credit, you need:

  • A final credit score of at least 650
  • A debt-to-income ratio of 50% or less

Improving Your Eligibility

If you're not currently eligible for credit, here are some ways to improve your chances:

  • Complete your account verification
  • Improve your credit score by paying bills on time
  • Reduce existing debt to improve your debt-to-income ratio
  • Ensure your income information is up-to-date
